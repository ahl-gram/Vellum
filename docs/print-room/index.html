This is the atelier's print room, where a world leaves the screen for the page. Bring a chart in from the Explorer, or call up a seed by number, and pull a proof to see how it sits on paper.

Your browser could not start the render worker, so proofs are drawn on the main thread. A large plate may pause the page for a moment.

Order a plate

Take this proof to a poster-size sheet in two steps: choose how it is pressed, then the plate size. The press pulls the same world wide and hands it to you to send to the printer.

Step two: choose the plate size

A PNG is pressed with your own device's fonts, so a chart may set a touch differently from machine to machine. Keep the vector SVG for an exact archival copy; a large image is fitted down to what your browser can hold.

The bound atlas

Bind the whole atlas of this world, the way the survey office would: the world chart and its other draughtings, the thematic surveys, the regional plates, the banners of the realms, the chronicle, and the gazetteer, all on one sheet. Then print it to PDF, or take the single self-contained file home.

Printing opens your browser's own Save as PDF, so choose your paper size there; one plate falls on each page. The single file is one self-contained page of about 20 MB that opens offline in any browser, with every plate carried inside it.
